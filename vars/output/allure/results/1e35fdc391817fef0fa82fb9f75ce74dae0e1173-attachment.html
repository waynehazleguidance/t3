Your Privacy

When you visit the Benefit Cosmetics website cookies may be placed on your device, subject to your browser settings or other cookie preferences you may have set. Cookies are small text files sent from a website and stored on your device. Some cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and others improve your browsing experience by remembering your preferences. Other cookies may track how you use a website to provide more relevant communications or marketing, or to better understand how the website is used. Some cookies are issued directly by Benefit Cosmetics or its third-party service providers while others may be issued by third-party companies. Only the entity issuing a cookie is able to read or modify the information they contain. In this Cookie Preference Center, you may find information about the first or third party cookies that may be placed on your device when you visit the benefitcosmetics.com website and the choices you can make.
