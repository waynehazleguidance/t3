Your Privacy

When you visit the Benefit Cosmetics website cookies may be placed on your device, subject to your browser settings or other cookie preferences you may have set. Cookies are small text files sent from a website and stored on your device. Some cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and others improve your browsing experience by remembering your preferences. Other cookies may track how you use a website to provide more relevant communications or marketing, or to better understand how the website is used. Some cookies are issued directly by Benefit Cosmetics or its third-party service providers while others may be issued by third-party companies. Only the entity issuing a cookie is able to read or modify the information they contain. In this Cookie Preference Center, you may find information about the first or third party cookies that may be placed on your device when you visit the benefitcosmetics.com website and the choices you can make. More information

Essential Cookies

Always Active

Essential cookies are necessary to enable you to browse our website and use its features. These cookies may be set by Benefit Cosmetics or by its service providers. For example, essential cookies enable you to use services such as placing online orders.

Analytical & Customization Cookies

Active

Analytical and customization cookies are not necessary for the functioning of our website, but they may improve your browsing experience by storing your preferences. For example, these cookies may help you to search more effectively, optimize your purchasing experience, help us better identify your requirements, improve our offers to you, and optimize the functioning of our website. Depending on your browser settings, information from analytical & customization cookies may be shared with third parties who provide services to us, such as analytics, advertising and social media partners.

Advertising & Targeting Cookies

Active

Advertising & targeting cookies are not essential for the use of our website. Advertising cookies, pixels and other similar technologies may be used to show you advertising that is more relevant to you. Depending on your browser settings, information from advertising & targeting cookies may be shared with third parties who provide services to us, such as analytics, advertising, and social media partners.
