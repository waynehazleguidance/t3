Details & Features

Details

A next-generation hair dryer that offers rapid drying, customizable styling and exceptional results. Cura LUXE utilizes T3 Digital IonAir technology for precise heat and a wide, gentle airstream that dries hair quickly and delivers soft, frizz-free results. It’s also equipped with multiple heat/speed settings, a Volume Booster switch, and Auto Pause Sensor that intuitively halts airflow. This miracle tool is destined for cult status.

  • Winner of six editorial beauty awards, T3 Cura Luxe is a hair game-changer
  • T3 IonAir Technology with digitally-controlled heat dries hair quickly while maintaining body and shine
  • 5 Heat & 2 Speed Settings offer styling customization for all hair types and textures
  • Volume Booster Switch boosts texture and volume*
  • Auto Pause Sensor intuitively pauses airflow when you release the dryer handle
  • Lock-in Cool Shot Button locks in any style for a smooth, sleek finish
  • Lightweight design and quiet operation for a luxurious drying experience
  • Ergonomic Slant Handle helps alleviate wrist strain for effortless drying
  • Pure Stream Cap removable filter for optimal performance
  • Drying Concentrator dries hair evenly and quickly with a wide volume of air
  • Styling Concentrator styles one section at a time with concentrated airflow
  • Long Life Motor ensures a reliable performance
  • 2,7m Cord with Cord Wrap offers professional-length cord for ease of use
  • Compatible with the SoftCurl Diffuser

*Results may vary by hair type.
